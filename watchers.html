Are there Watchers?

Food 3rd January, 2019. • 3

This is to those who are fools. Those who are fools and are enslaved with persuasion of what they believe. This is to those who are so addicted and in love with something, they give thier very last. This is to those who have ever beheld sacrifice in the sense of ever giving and never receiving. Yet they still give and never receive.

Some call them the unsang heros. I have known in an overflowing level of knowledge what it means to be a true unsang hero. And I hear the critics! "You sound so pathetic! You should not want to be sang! Don't be in a place you are not sang to the world!"

This is to those who have no honour. To those who are weak enough to have no voice. This is to the unsang heros!

I have seen many times how these unsang heros sink like they were never heros. Quickly forgotten and never remembered. Buried in the lowest depths in a funeral no one attended. There was no one to see their scares from the sword fights. No one to carry them from the battle field where their last energy drained...

Maybe if there were watchers, they wouldn't have sank this deep so fast. If there were people who gave them water each time they walked miles to make sure our needs were catered for, we would still have many heros around. Answers why people are scared of being heros. Some are at crossroads of either being unsang or not composed.

These magical people have the tricks that are the very needs of nations but we dont sing them loud enough. I know of a hero who is not sang loud enough. Relentlessly, Recklessly giving the greatest of all gifts and even with a super power of seeing all at a glance, is not seen enough, to be sang loud enough.

So I won't relent when I'm not sang loud enough or not sang at all. Besides, I have 1 loyal follower on my watcher's channel who is betther than a million ones and He is going to sing me loud enough to everything that matters when its the time is right.

Am I an unsang hero? Yes! Are there watchers? No! There is just one watcher who is also an unsang hero and I believe in tit for tat, or in karma or in the truth of doing unto others what I want them to do unto me. So I'm going to sing Him so loud by fighting so hard, that He sings me so loud!

#UnapologeticallyUnsang
